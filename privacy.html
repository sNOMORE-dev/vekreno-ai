About

Built to make exam preparation simpler.

NO MORE is an exam paper generator and student dashboard designed to help users create, save, track, and review study material in one place.

Platform story

Why NO MORE exists.

Simple creation

Users can enter a subject, topic, difficulty, and question count to quickly generate a paper.

Persistent storage

Saved exam papers, points, and history can stay available in the browser using localStorage.

Clear tracking

A dashboard, points page, and activity page make it easy to understand user progress at a glance.

Design principles

Focused, fast, and easy to expand.

Fast to use

The site uses simple HTML pages and small, direct interactions so users can move quickly between features.

Easy to maintain

Each page has a clear job, which makes the project easier to edit, debug, and grow later.

Ready for future upgrades

The app can later add real databases, authentication, payment gateways, and analytics without changing the overall structure.
